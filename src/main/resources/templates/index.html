Recycling plastic is crucial for preserving our environment and combating pollution. Plastic waste poses a significant threat to ecosystems, wildlife, and human health. By recycling plastic, we can reduce the amount of waste sent to landfills and minimize the need for new plastic production, which requires valuable resources and contributes to greenhouse gas emissions.

About Us

By using special sorting equipment and engaging new volunteers, we can achieve our goal. Don't stand aside - recycle bottles and polyethylene bags.
